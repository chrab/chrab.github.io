My name is Christian Rab. I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Group of Barbara Ercolano at the Universtitätssternwared München [USM](https://www.usm.uni-muenchen.de/) and the LMU (Munich, Germany).


My main interest is star and planet formation. To understand the (initial) conditions for planet-formation I use observations (e.g. ALMA) and model the circumstellar environment (disks, envelopes) of young stars using the radiation thermo-chemical modelling code [ProDiMo](https://www.astro.rug.nl/~prodimo/).
