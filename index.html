My name is Christian Rab. I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Group of Inga Kamp at the Kapteyn Astronomical Institute of the University of Groningen (Netherlands).

My main interest is star and planet formation. To understand the (initial) conditions for planet-formation I use observations (e.g. ALMA) and model the circumstellar environment (disks, envelopes) of young stars using the radiation thermo-chemical modelling code ProDiMo.
